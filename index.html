TECENOCRUISE by TECHKRITI and IEI SCCSE

IEI Students’ Chapter CSE is going to organise a technical workshop on Internet of Things (IOT) and Ethical Hacking collaborated with IIT Kanpur. The Schedule of TECHNOCRUISE is divided into two phases. On 3rd and 4th March,2023 the professors of IIT Kanpur will conduct two workshops and on 11th March the competition will conduct at the auditorium of our college.

The Benefits

This workshop will provide practical experience on IOT and Ethical Hacking.The innovative ideas on projects will help everyone to take perfect decisions in every unusual situation.The Participations in competition enhance your skills on this fields.

Perks

  • • The winners of the competition will get the free tickets for the Tech Fest of IIT Kanpur.

  • • Each and every participant can get the certificate from IIT Kanpur and as well as from our college.

  • • Each and every participant can get the certificate from IIT Kanpur and as well as from our college.

    • Registration

    • If any participant wants to attend any one of the workshop then he/she should pay 450 rupees as his registration and participation fee.
    • If anyone wants to attends both of the workshops that means 3rd March and 4th March both, then there is a package of 850 rupees to attend both workshops.
